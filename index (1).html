Research Experience

Ongoing Research

Self-correcting LQR Algorithm for Robust Control Systems

Independent Research | 2024 - Present

Developing a self-correcting algorithm for Linear Quadratic Regulator (LQR) control systems subjected to adversarial database attacks. The algorithm integrates convex optimization via semi-definite programming with uncertainty-weighted regression, enabling systems to actively identify and adapt to improper perturbations in data. This work demonstrates that robust systems require not just accuracy, but intrinsic self-correction capabilities to navigate uncertain environments.

Control Theory Convex Optimization Robustness Bandits
